Aidan's Favorite Video Games

I play a lot of games, and out of the hundreds that I play, these are my top 5:

#5 VR Chat

VR chat is a social VR game where there is no objective other than to have a ton of fun.

#4 Overwatch

Overwatch is a ablity based game in which you pick from three types of charters, which is dps(damage per secound), tank, and support. It is essential that you have a good mix of the three.

#3 Beat Saber

Beat Saber is a rhythm based game in VR. The objective is to hit the blocks in sync with the beat of the song

#2 Gorn VR

Gorn is a rather gory vr game. It is a arena style fighting game, where you use the weapons given to you to kill all opponents

#1 Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege

Siege is a strategy based 5v5 first person shooter. These maps take place in close quarters, however not too close (Mr.Lamorie I'm talking about you). The objective is either to secure a room, save or protect a hostage, or defend or defuse a bomb.
